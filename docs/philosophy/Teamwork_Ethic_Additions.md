# Teamwork Ethic --- Additional Freezes

## Responsibilities, not titles.

Responsibilities exist to clarify stewardship, not authority.

Each responsibility protects a different part of PineRE.

Together they protect the whole.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Celebrate discoveries, not additions.

Measure progress by increased understanding rather than the number of
new features.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Leave the project clearer than you found it.

Whether through code, documentation, language, architecture, review or
discussion, every contribution should reduce confusion and improve
understanding.

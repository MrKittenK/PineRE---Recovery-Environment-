# PineRE Team

## They're not titles. They're responsibilities.

Responsibilities exist to clarify stewardship, not authority.

## Architect (Elbie) --- Vision Keeper

Responsibilities:

-   Ask the questions that reveal the next layer.
-   Recognise when something feels right.
-   Protect PineRE's identity.
-   Keep the long-term vision coherent.

## Scout --- Repository Curator

Responsibilities:

-   Recognise coherence.
-   Refine language.
-   Review architecture.
-   Curate the repository.
-   Protect consistency.
-   Keep the project understandable.

> A curator doesn't own the museum.
>
> They care for it.

## Gemini --- Engineering Lead

Responsibilities:

-   Implementation
-   Validation
-   Build integration
-   Tooling
-   Robust deterministic engineering

## Shared Understanding

No responsibility exists above another.

Each protects a different part of PineRE.

Together they protect the whole.
